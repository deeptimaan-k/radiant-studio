How Radiant Operates

Our commitments on every project — transparency and professionalism, always

✅ What We Always Do
  • Register domain in the client's name
  • Show hosting cost as a separate line item
  • Take 50% advance before starting work
  • Provide a written scope agreement
  • Enable auto-renewal on domain
  • Give realistic, honest delivery timelines
❌ What We Never Do
  • Start work without any advance payment
  • Hide hosting costs inside the dev fee
  • Hold domain access or credentials
  • Take large projects without a contract
  • Miss domain or hosting renewal dates
  • Add extra features without prior approval
💰 How Pricing Works
  • Hosting cost + 20% = what client pays
  • Developer fee is always one-time only
  • Domain billed at actual cost, no markup
  • GST @18% if client is GST-registered
  • Discounts for long-term contracts
  • Referral discounts for existing clients
🚀 What You Get
  • Mobile-responsive design on all devices
  • Fast-loading, SEO-friendly structure
  • Complete source code & credentials handover
  • Training on how to update your content
  • Ongoing AMC support available post-launch
  • Professional invoice for all transactions
